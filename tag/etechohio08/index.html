Low tech and high tech communication - eTech Ohio 2008

Sat 15 March 2008
By mr.rcollins

In blog.

[![The audience #4][]][]At the eTech Ohio Technology Conference this past February I presented on different ways a person can communicate. Some ways were very low tech (writing a letter, using the phone), and some ways very high tech (using Grand Central to control incoming calls, social networking with del.icio.us or Twitter).
